## Script 3: Objection Handling Playbook

### Objection 1: "The government takes too long to pay."
* **Response:** "I understand that concern. Actually, the federal government passed the *Prompt Payment Act*, which legally obligates federal agencies to pay small business invoices within 15 days of approval, often faster than commercial corporate clients who push for 60 or 90 days."

### Objection 2: "Your consulting fees are too high."
* **Response:** "I hear you, and it is an investment. But think of it this way: a single average federal sole-source award under the 8(a) program is worth over $150,000. Our fee represents less than 10% of your first win. We also provide full administrative and compliance auditing, saving you hundreds of hours of trial and error."

### Objection 3: "I can register on SAM.gov for free myself."
* **Response:** "You absolutely can. But over 60% of self-registered businesses make errors in their NAICS code mappings, CAGE code validations, or capability Statements. A single registration mistake can disqualify you from a multi-million dollar award before your proposal is even read. We ensure your profile is optimized to actually attract contract officers."
