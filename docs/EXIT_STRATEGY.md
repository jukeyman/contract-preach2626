# 🚪 EXIT STRATEGY & ACQUISITION ROADMAP
**RJ Business Solutions: Strategic Valuation Model**

---

## 1. Overview
The Contracting Preacher division is built from day one to operate as a highly scalable, systematized, and transferrable enterprise. By decoupling client acquisitions and fulfillment from Dr. McKnight's individual time—achieved via the **AI Command Center** and standard operating procedures (SOPs)—the division can be seamlessly acquired by a third-party consulting conglomerate, federal technology platform, or private equity firm.

## 2. Valuation Triggers & Milestones
We target acquisition at a **5x to 7x EBITDA multiple** based on reaching the following recurring milestones:

* **Milestone 1 (Seed Scale):** 50 active monthly command center subscribers ($15,000 MRR) + $1.2 million annual consulting revenue.
* **Milestone 2 (SaaS Acceleration):** 200 command center subscribers ($60,000 MRR) + $3.5 million annual consulting revenue.
* **Milestone 3 (Acquisition Ready):** 500 command center subscribers ($150,000 MRR) + $8 million annual consulting revenue (EBITDA margin maintained at >45%).

## 3. Potential Acquirers
* **Federal Technology Platforms:** Companies looking to integrate high-ticket coaching/consulting into their existing procurement software.
* **National Consulting Conglomerates:** National accounting or professional services firms seeking to add a dedicated federal set-aside certification wing.
* **Private Equity Firms:** Specialist firms that acquire government service providers and roll them up into larger, unified federal agencies.

## 4. Transition & Wind-Down Procedures
Upon execution of an acquisition term sheet:
1. **Intellectual Property Transfer:** Transfer ownership of the Next.js/Cloudflare Pages codebase, D1 schemas, and local/remote AI configurations.
2. **Consulting Advisory Transition:** Dr. McKnight will transition into a 6-month Chief Strategic Advisor role to facilitate high-value client introductions.
3. **Operational Hand-off:** Hand over all SOP operational matrices, sales training materials, calling scripts, and staff manuals to the acquiring team.
