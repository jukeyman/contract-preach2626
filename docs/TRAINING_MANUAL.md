## Module 1: Foundational Federal Frameworks
As a consultant at The Contracting Preacher, you must understand the basic nomenclature of government procurement:
* **FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation):** The codification of uniform policies and procedures for acquisition by all executive agencies.
* **SAM.gov (System for Award Management):** The official U.S. Government website where vendors register to do business with the government.
* **UEI (Unique Entity Identifier):** A 12-character alphanumeric ID assigned to an entity by SAM.gov.
* **CAGE Code:** A five-character ID assigned by the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) to identify commercial and government entities.

## Module 2: Understanding SBA Socio-Economic Certifications
You must memorize the core qualifying criteria for certifications to confidently advise prospects:

### 1. SBA 8(a) Business Development Program
* **Target:** Socially and economically disadvantaged individuals.
* **Key Thresholds:** Personal net worth < $850,000; adjusted gross income < $400,000; total assets < $6.5 million.
* **Duration:** 9-year maximum term.

### 2. Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB)
* **Target:** Small businesses unconditionally owned and controlled by one or more women.
* **Key Thresholds:** 51% direct ownership, managed by a woman on a full-time basis.

### 3. HUBZone (Historically Underutilized Business Zone)
* **Target:** Businesses located in and employing residents of economically distressed communities.
* **Key Thresholds:** Principal office must be in a HUBZone; at least 35% of all employees must reside in a HUBZone.

### 4. SDVOSB (Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business)
* **Target:** Small businesses owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans.
* **Key Thresholds:** 51% ownership and daily business operations managed by a disabled veteran.

## Module 3: Consultative Discovery Process
When speaking with small business owners, never start by "selling" consulting packages. Instead, lead with diagnostics:
1. **Diagnosis:** Ask about their current revenue, capability STATEMENT, and current certifications.
2. **Analysis:** Identify their gaps (e.g., lack of active bids, non-compliant registrations).
3. **Prescription:** Recommend a clear roadmap (SAM.gov repair → SBA Certification → AI Command Center onboarding).
