# ⚙️ STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOP)
**The Contracting Preacher Operations Manual**

---

## SOP-001: Client Intake and Prequalification
* **Objective:** Standardize the evaluation and grading of new inbound prospects.
* **Owner:** Intake Consultant

### Procedure:
1. Direct the prospect to fill out the form at `/intake`.
2. Review the business metrics against SBA requirements:
   * **Gross Revenues:** Confirm three-year average is under industry size standards.
   * **Ownership:** Ensure at least 51% ownership by U.S. citizens of target socio-economic categories.
   * **NAICS Codes:** Identify primary 6-digit NAICS code.
3. Check SAM.gov status using our integrated tools.
4. Schedule a 30-minute introductory call only if the prospect meets the eligibility threshold.

---

## SOP-002: Socio-Economic Certification Filing
* **Objective:** Ensure error-free prep and submission of SBA certification packages.
* **Owner:** Certification Specialist

### Procedure:
1. Collect all corporate documents from the client (tax returns, articles of organization, resumes).
2. For **8(a) Applicants:** Verify personal net worth of the disadvantaged individual is under $850,000.
3. For **HUBZone Applicants:** Confirm principal office is in a HUBZone and at least 35% of employees reside in a HUBZone.
4. Input all structured business metrics into the **AI Command Center** to identify any compliance risks.
5. Compile and submit the final package through the SBA certification portal.

---

## SOP-003: Bid Draft & Compliance Audit
* **Objective:** Execute consistent, compliant, and high-scoring proposal development.
* **Owner:** Proposal Writer / AI Operator

### Procedure:
1. Locate the federal opportunity ID on the command center (`/agent`).
2. Generate the first technical draft using the **Drafting Panel** in the AI Command Center.
3. Review the draft in the **Audit Panel** to cross-examine against FAR/DFARS compliance metrics and request guidelines.
4. Correct any critical compliance warnings identified by the audit.
5. Export the final proposal draft and submit to Dr. McKnight for final executive sign-off.

---

## SOP-004: Escalation & Advisory Handoffs
* **Objective:** Ensure seamless routing of high-value disputes or complex certification cases.
* **Owner:** Account Manager

### Procedure:
1. Identify cases involving complicated ownership structures or previous SBA application rejections.
2. Flag the CRM record as "High Priority - Dr. McKnight Review".
3. Prepare a concise case summary in the workspace documents.
4. Transition the case file to Dr. McKnight's office for a dedicated executive strategy session.
